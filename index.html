About Me

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student in the Economics department at Rice University in Houston, Texas. My research interests include urban economics, transportation economics, and machine learning methods.

Prior to my Ph.D, I received a Bachelor of Arts from McGill University in Montreal, Quebec, and was a predoctoral researcher at Kellogg School of Management at Northwestern University in Evanston, Illinois.
